In order to make chicken follow these steps:

  1. chop one onion and fry it in your pan until it turns brown.
  2. add chicken pieces to your pan, lower the heat, close the lid and let them fry for some time.
  3. open the lid and add tomato sauce and chopped potatos to the pan.
  4. add boiling water to the mix, and close the lid again
  5. wait for 45 mins, then its ready.
