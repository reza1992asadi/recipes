In order to make pan-kebab follow these steps:

  1. crush one onion and mix it with minced meat(you can use your hand)
  2. you don't need any oil for this.
  3. spread the mixture over the surface of the pan, spread it all over the pan, make sure its now a thin layor
  4. decrease the heat to the lowest and wait till the water in the pan evaporates.
  5. then put the whole pan inside the oven and let it be there for 15 mins with 100 degrees.
  6. take out the pan and enjoy your food.
