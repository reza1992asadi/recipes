In order to make pasta follow these steps:

  1. boil the pasta for 8 minutes in water.
  2. chup one onion into small pieces
  3. put the chopped onion into a pan and fry them with low heat
  4. add your meat to the onions and cook them together for 30 minutes
  5. add tomato sauce and spices.
  6. add the pasta to the mix and mix them up.
